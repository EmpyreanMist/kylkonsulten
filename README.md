## Kylkonsulten i Norr AB

Kylkonsulten i Norr AB is a Swedish training and examination provider within refrigeration and cooling technology.

The company specializes in **theoretical and practical examinations on behalf of INCERT**, as well as **preparatory courses for Category 1 and 2 refrigeration certification**. All training is designed for professionals with technical backgrounds such as electricians, HVAC technicians, or individuals with hands-on experience in refrigeration systems.

Services include:
- INCERT examinations (theoretical and practical)
- Preparatory courses for Category 1 & 2 certification
- Refresher and recertification courses
- Customized training based on prior experience

Training is delivered with a strong focus on real-world competence, regulatory compliance, and professional readiness.
